India Culture

Indian culture is unique and has its traditional value, religion, dance, music, cuisine, dressing, and more1. It is the oldest culture in the world1. Indian culture is known for its shishtata, vinamrata, and ideals2. India is a cluster of cultures that cannot be found anywhere else1. Indian culture encompasses the language, religion, and traditions of millions of Indians3. Indian people are welcoming and learn to live in harmony with others through experiencing cultural diversity.
